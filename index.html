Congressman Bob Gibbs

Bob Gibbs is the representative of Ohio's 7th congressional district, and has served as a Republican since January of 2013. He was previously serving the 18th district from 2011-2012.

Senator Gibbs is currently serving on a number of committees and subcommittees which are listed below.

List of committees

  • House of Agriculture
  • House on Transportation and Infastructure

List of sub-committees

  • Biotechnology, Horticulture and Research
  • General Farm Committees and Risk Management
  • Aviation
  • Highways and Transit
  • Water Resources and Environment

List of Enacted Legislations

  • Ensuring American Voters Act of 2018
  • Protecting Businesses from Burdensome Compliance Cost Act of 2018
  • Expressing the sense of the House of Representatives that clean water is a national priority, and that the June 29, 2015, Waters of the United States Rule should be withdrawn or vacated.
